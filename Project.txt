parse through excel files in a folder and output a summary into an HTML document. 

The summary should include the following calculations:
Total Impressions (The sum of everything from the Impr. column)
Filled Impressions (Total impressions - Passback Impressions from the Impr. column)
Earnings (Sum of everything in the Rev. column divided by two)
eCPM (Earnings / (Total Impressions / 1000))

The template to output to has been included, and the calculations have been done for Report 1 for clarity.
